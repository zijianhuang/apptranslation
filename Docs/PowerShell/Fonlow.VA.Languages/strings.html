ScoreHelp Visual acuity notations of Snellen Fractions 20/20, 5/5, or 6/6, and Decimal 1.0 refer to the reference standard. Equivalent notations include MAR 1.0, LogMAR 0 and Visual Acuity Score 100. “Normal” visual acuity for healthy eyes is 1 or 2 lines better than Decimal 1.0 or Visual Acuity Score 100. Visual Acuity Score (VAS) is more intuitive since higher integer values indicate better visual acuity. VAS value lower than 100 may indicate myopia, however, only optometrists or ophthalmologists can diagnose myopia or pseudo myopia. Early diagnosis and preventive measures may prevent pseudo myopia from becoming myopia, or prevent myopia from becoming worse. Checking visual acuity weekly, fortnightly or monthly may help early detection and diagnosis.
